# Login Troubleshooting Guide

This guide helps users solve common login problems.

## Problem 1: Incorrect Password

### Cause

The password entered is incorrect.

### Solution

1. Click "Forgot Password"
2. Enter your email address
3. Follow the reset instructions

---

## Problem 2: Internet Connection Issue

### Cause

The device is not connected to the internet.

### Solution

1. Check your internet connection
2. Restart your router
3. Try logging in again

---

## Problem 3: Browser Issue

### Cause

The browser may have cached outdated data.

### Solution

1. Clear browser cache
2. Restart the browser
3. Try logging in again
